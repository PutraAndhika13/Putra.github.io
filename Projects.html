Project 1: Digital IT Service Channel

Digital IT Service Channel is a portfolio project designed to educate and assist a general audience in navigating IT challenges. Through step-by-step tutorials, practical tips & tricks, and comprehensive troubleshooting guides, the channel demonstrates how AI tools (Claude, Grok) and content platforms (YouTube) can be leveraged to provide accessible IT support.

The project showcases real-world problem-solving approaches, emphasizing practical value and making complex IT concepts understandable for users of all skill levels. Over the past 2 months, the channel has served as both a learning platform and a portfolio piece, highlighting the potential of AI-assisted content creation for technical education.
